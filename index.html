About Matka Desi Delights

At Matka Desi Delights, we believe in bringing the authentic taste of India to your glass. Our milkshakes are crafted with traditional recipes, using fresh ingredients and served in eco-friendly matkas (clay pots) that enhance the flavor and keep your drink cool.

Founded in 2020, we've been serving happiness in a glass with our unique desi twist on classic milkshakes. Come visit us and experience the taste of tradition!
